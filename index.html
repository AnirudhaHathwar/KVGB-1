ABOUT KVGB

Krishna Vilas Grameena Bank (KVGB) is a Regional Rural Bank (RRB) formed on 12-Aug-2019. The major focus of the bank is to initiate appropriate intervention for overall economic upliftment and advancement of the people of Karnataka by giving financial assistance to the sectors like Agriculture, Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, education, housing, micro-credit, weaker sections etc.

We understand that all customers are different in unique ways, which is why, regardless of the size of your business or your aspirations, we treat everyone as individual and special. This means offering you options not only in relation to our services but also in the way you interact with us. We understand the changes in your lifestyle and support you with our services.

We have deployed the most modern information technology to deliver our services for your benefits with an aim to develop an effective long-term relationship. Our business is more than banking because we touch the lives of people in many ways.
